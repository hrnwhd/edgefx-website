Frequently Asked Questions

What is copy trading? +
Copy trading allows you to automatically replicate trades from an experienced trader's account to your own account. When we open a trade, your account automatically opens the same trade proportionally. You maintain full control and can stop copying anytime.
How much do I need to start? +
Minimum $100 for Tier 1 (Cent Aggressive). For $1,000-$3,000, we recommend Tier 2 (Cent Conservative). For $3,000+, Tier 3 (Standard Account) will be available in March 2025.

What are the risks? +
Forex trading involves substantial risk of loss. While we employ sophisticated risk management, losses can and do occur. Never trade with money you cannot afford to lose. Our historical performance does not guarantee future results. Always follow the 50-40-10 rule and only use risk capital.
Can I stop copying anytime? +
Yes, absolutely. You maintain full control of your account. You can pause copying, stop completely, or adjust settings at any time through the Social Trader Tools platform.
